## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Authentication

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: You can sign up using your email address and password, or use Apple Sign In or Google Sign In for quick registration.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Currently, email addresses cannot be changed after account creation. Please contact support if you need assistance.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**  
A: Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

**Q: I'm having trouble signing in with Apple/Google.**  
A: Make sure you're using the same authentication method you used to create your account. If issues persist, try logging out of your Apple/Google account on your device and signing in again.

### Learning & Progress

**Q: How does the learning path work?**  
A: Each sport has multiple modules, and each module contains lessons. Complete lessons in order to unlock the next one. Earn XP and improve your overall rating as you progress!

**Q: What happens if I get a question wrong?**  
A: Don't worry! You'll see the correct answer and an explanation. Wrong answers help you learn, and you can review concepts later through the Review feature.

**Q: How is my overall rating calculated?**  
A: Your rating (0-99) is based on lessons completed, accuracy, concepts mastered, live answers, and advanced content. Keep learning to improve your rating!

**Q: What is XP and how do I earn it?**  
A: XP (Experience Points) is earned by:
- Completing lesson questions correctly (10 XP each)
- Perfect lesson scores (bonus 20 XP)
- Live game answers (15 XP)
- Daily streaks (25 XP)
- Review questions (5 XP)

**Q: Can I skip lessons?**  
A: No, lessons must be completed in order to ensure you build a strong foundation. Each lesson unlocks the next one.

**Q: How long are lessons?**  
A: Most lessons take 3-5 minutes to complete and contain 8-12 questions.

### Gamification & Leaderboards

**Q: How do badges work?**  
A: Badges are earned by achieving specific milestones, such as completing your first lesson, maintaining a 7-day streak, or reaching certain XP levels.

**Q: How do leaderboards work?**  
A: Leaderboards show top users based on XP earned. You can view daily, weekly, and all-time rankings for each sport.

**Q: Can I compete with friends?**  
A: Currently, leaderboards are global. Friend-based competition features are planned for future updates!

### Live Mode

**Q: What is Live Mode?**  
A: Live Mode lets you answer real-time questions during live sports games. Get prompts during key moments and test your knowledge as the action unfolds!

**Q: When is Live Mode available?**  
A: Live Mode is active during scheduled live games. Check the Live tab to see upcoming games.

**Q: How do I enable Live Mode notifications?**  
A: Go to Profile → Settings → Notifications and enable "Live Game Alerts."

### Review & Spaced Repetition

**Q: What is the Review feature?**  
A: Review uses spaced repetition to help you retain what you've learned. Questions you've seen before will reappear at optimal intervals to strengthen your memory.

**Q: How often should I review?**  
A: Check the Review tab daily to see if you have any due reviews. The system automatically schedules reviews based on your performance.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app is crashing or freezing.**  
A: Try these steps:
1. Force quit the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Check for app updates in the App Store
4. If the issue persists, contact support with details about when the crash occurs

**Q: I'm not receiving notifications.**  
A: Check:
1. App notification settings: Profile → Settings → Notifications
2. Device settings: Settings → Notifications → SportsIQ
3. Ensure "Do Not Disturb" is not enabled

**Q: My progress isn't syncing.**  
A: Make sure you have an active internet connection. Progress is saved to the cloud automatically when you're online.

**Q: The app is running slowly.**  
A: Try:
1. Closing other apps running in the background
2. Restarting your device
3. Ensuring you have the latest iOS version
4. Freeing up device storage

**Q: I found a bug. How do I report it?**  
A: Please email us at noah@nsgsolutions.co with:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Device model and iOS version
- Screenshots (if applicable)

## Troubleshooting Guide

### Can't Sign In
1. Verify you're using the correct email and password
2. Check if Caps Lock is enabled
3. Try resetting your password
4. Ensure you're using the same sign-in method (email, Apple, or Google) you used to create your account

### Missing Progress
1. Confirm you're signed in to the correct account
2. Check your internet connection
3. Pull down to refresh the app
4. Sign out and sign back in

### Notifications Not Working
1. Enable notifications in app settings
2. Enable notifications in device settings
3. Ensure the app is updated to the latest version
4. Restart your device

### Performance Issues
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your device
3. Update to the latest iOS version
4. Update to the latest app version
5. Free up device storage (at least 1GB recommended)
